# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Plant operators and engineers monitoring equipment health, alongside facility managers tracking high-level economic and energy costs.

## Product Purpose
A cloud-native visual digital twin for Municipal/Industrial BWRO facilities that unifies operational data, physics simulation, AI diagnostics, and economics to provide real-time, evidence-based monitoring and forecasting.

## Brand Personality
Utilitarian, Precise, Trustworthy

## Anti-references
Generic SaaS dashboards, overly colorful interfaces, 'AI slop' templates with soft drop-shadows, excessive gradients, and overused rounded pills.

## Design Principles
- **Data Provenance is Paramount**: Every quantitative metric must display its source (measured vs modeled) and baseline comparison.
- **Expert Confidence**: The UI should respect the user's intelligence by prioritizing dense, actionable information over decorative elements.
- **Form Follows Function**: Layouts must serve the workflow. Limit decorative color to state changes (nominal, degraded, critical) and let the data speak.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard contrast (WCAG AA) requirements must be met to ensure legibility across industrial and office screens.
